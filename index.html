Dhyan's G.T Project

Introduction

Welcome to my GT (Gifted & Talented) project! The goal of this project is to explore various colleges and career paths, understand the costs and requirements for each, and see how education choices can shape long-term success. By researching top universities, career clusters, and salary progressions, I aim to learn how different paths connect to professional opportunities. This project also allows me to analyze personal interests and create a clear flowchart for my own career plan, helping me visualize the journey from education to my dream career in management consulting.
